Bili is a bilingual app designed for children 3-8 who are learning multiple languages or want to strengthen their Spanish and English in a fun and educational way. Bili offers stories, games, songs, and cultural content that celebrate the richness of Latin@ communities while supporting your child’s language development and cultural identity.

You’re invited to join our one (1) month pilot program and be among the first to experience Bili. Your family will get free, early access, and by sharing feedback, help shape the app before its official release. Families who complete the pilot will also receive a $50 gift card!
