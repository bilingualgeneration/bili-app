Make Bili part of your child’s daily routine! You can play together and explore bilingual stories and educational activities, or share a bedtime story in Spanish or English. Bili features a rich variety of stories and voices from across Latin America and the diaspora, offering your child a unique opportunity to connect with diverse ways of being and sounding Latin@.
